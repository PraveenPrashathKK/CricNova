01 — FIELD

CRICKET FIELD

A cricket field is a large circular or oval ground with a 22-yard pitch at its center.It is strategically divided into various fielding positions that play a crucial rolein both batting and bowling tactics. The pitch is where the bowler delivers the balland the batsman attempts to score runs. Surrounding areas such as the infield and outfield influence how runs are scored and prevented. Field placements are adjusted based on the match situation, player strengths, and game format.

02 — FORMATS

CRICKET FORMATS

Cricket is played in three distinct formats, each with its own character,strategy, and thrill. From the five-day battle of Test cricket to the explosive twenty overs of T20, every format demands a different skill set and mindset from players.
